Buenaventura Durruti

"From my earliest years, the first thing I saw around me was the suffering, not only our family's suffering but also of our neighbors' suffering. By intuition, I was already a rebel. I believe that then my destiny was decided."

A hero for many, an assasin for some. He was both, also a milician, a mechanic and an anarcho-syndicalist. But among everything he was, he was a man fighting for freedom. And during Spanish Civil War, that meant killing other men.
